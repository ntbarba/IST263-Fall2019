IST263 Project Idea

The Whitman Faculty Network

Description

This website will serve the purpose of showing Whitman faculty how much of them know each other directly and indirectly. A study in by Stanley Milgram in 1967 demonstrated to the world that everyone is connected through 6 degrees of separation. In 2008, O2 concluded that the number is now closer to 3. This project aims to show faculty that although they may not know every colleague, the school is one big webbed family. The project will include short interviews of staff, a picture, and asking them 3 people they know. I will interview those 3 people, and so on.

Pages

Home
Will include a search factulty option, a short description about the project, and a button to start with a random faculty member.
Faculty
Will be a list (including pictures) of all the faculty involved in the project in alphabetical order.
About
Will talk about the project and go into detail about the 6 degrees of separation (which many people say is now 3) concept.

Sourcing Content

All content produced and displayed on the website will be my own intellectual property and sourced through interviews. For example, all photos will be taken at the time of the short interview. Other images needed for the website design will be sourced through Google, ensuring they are lebeled for free reuse. Other information, including their answer to the interview question, "What brought you to Syracuse?", and the 3 people they know will also be sourced in the faculty interviews.
