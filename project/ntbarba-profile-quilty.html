What led me to Whitman?

"Towards the end of my college career, I realized that I always wanted to work in a college setting and preferably in advising. I pursued my Masters in Higher Education and lived in the DC area working for 2 well known Universities before moving to Syracuse in 2008. I have been in Whitman since 2009 and have worked my way from advisor to assistant dean; I get to work with students at a point in their life when optimism is at an all-time high which is infectious. I am so fortunate to be in this role!"

Lindsay Quilty

Assistant Dean for Undergraduate Programs
