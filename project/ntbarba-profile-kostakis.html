What led me to Whitman?

"I actually received a degree in Marketing from the SOM in 1991. One day I was up at Lake Ontario visiting friends and I met John Torrens. Since we are both entrepreneurs he was telling me about his position as a Professor of Practice at Whitman. It sounded amazing. He introduced me to a few people and after being an adjunct for one semester I was hooked!"

Alexandra Kostakis

Professor of Entrepreneurial Practice
